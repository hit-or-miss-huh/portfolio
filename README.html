What can I do on the GOBDASM?

The buttons at the center of the page (how you got here) are genuine links to my other pages, like my music account. The buttons at the bottom of the screen go unnoticed by most who visit, but include great images. However, that leaves out the most important part. The tabs at the top of the screen are the beef of the GOBDASM. With a wide variety of rhyming words, there are many options to choose from. These include: backstory, battlefields, glitches, a very specific kink, our merch page, and most importantly: the More tab, which takes you to almost any video imaginable.
